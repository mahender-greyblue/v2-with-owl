ABOUT US

Creating “Gen B” : A path breaking Socio-Digital approach revolutionizing South Asia's 1Bn blue collar workforce.

We are building Saathi, a comprehensive AI powered mobile ecosystem, empowering and uplifting blue collar workers become a future-ready skilled workforce, delivering higher productivity and value to their employers and also radically transforming their own lives through identity, skills, digital communities and financial inclusion.

The Saathi ecosystem is paving the way for Scalable Social Impact and an Inclusive, Equitable and Prosperous future for our workforce. With a Noble vision and a Novel approach, we have combined principles of Capitalism with the values of Socialism, embracing a Profit-with-Purpose business with long-term Sustainability and Social Responsibility, along with a focus on Value Creation.

It is our conviction that with the integration of Social Purpose with Commercial Acumen & Capital, businesses can emerge as powerful Agents of Change - delivering profits while positively Impacting Society and Making a Difference.

A better life for a billion people

  • Life Skill & Well Being

    Promoting Resilience, self-esteem, communication and other critical life skills, helping improve employability and contributing positively to the lives of workers.

  • Promoting financial inclusion, security & Freedom.

    Providing a gateway to Financial Inclusion & Security for Blue-Collar workers.

  • Preserving the social fabric of families with reduced labour migration.

    Reducing labour migration and breakup of families for earning livelihoods through in-demand job role skilling, enabling local employment.

  • Reducing inequality by income augmentation.

    Aspiring for every Blue-Collar household to augment its income and uplift its standard of living.

  • Women Empowerment & increased Workforce Inclusion.

    Increasing women’s skilling & participation in the workforce and empowering them to take charge of their lives.

  • Providing Identity & Dignity.

    Bringing transformative change in the lives of millions of Blue-Collar workers by providing them with a Verified Identity and a respectable and Better Life.
